How I Got This Opportunity

*First let me introduce MUN ?

Model United Nations (MUN) is a space where students step into the shoes of world leaders, representing countries and working together to solve global challenges. Instead of just reading about issues like climate change or human rights, you actually debate them, share your countrys views, and try to build common solutions with others. It is less about being perfect and more about learning how to listen, speak with confidence, and find common ground, even when opinions differ. Beyond the speeches and debates, MUN is about teamwork, leadership, and making friends from diverse backgrounds .It is a chance to grow as a person while practicing how young people like us can imagine and shape a better world.
